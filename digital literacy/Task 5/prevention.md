#  Prevention Checklist (Cyberbullying)

- Think before you post or comment online  
- Do not engage with bullies; avoid responding to negative comments  
- Block and report abusive users immediately  
- Keep your social media accounts private when possible  
- Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Save evidence (screenshots) of bullying messages  

## 💳 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Do not accept unknown payment requests or click suspicious links  

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
